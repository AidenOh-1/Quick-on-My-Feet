# Quick on My Feet MVP Product Spec

## Product thesis

People do not just need an AI writer. They need a social timing assistant that helps them reply quickly without sounding boring, awkward, too serious, or cringe.

## MVP hypothesis

If users can paste a message and receive 5 genuinely usable replies in under 10 seconds, they will use the product repeatedly for high-stakes social or professional conversations.

## Primary user jobs

1. Help me understand what the other person probably means.
2. Help me reply without overthinking.
3. Help me sound natural, witty, warm, or confident.
4. Help me avoid sounding cringe or too intense.
5. Let me copy a reply immediately.

## Initial segments

### Segment 1: Dating / social texting

Pain: Users want to sound interesting without trying too hard.

### Segment 2: International professionals

Pain: Users know English but struggle with nuance, humor, warmth, or casual tone.

### Segment 3: Group chat users

Pain: Users want quick comebacks that are funny but not mean.

## Success metrics for user testing

- Copy rate: % of generated sessions where user copies a reply
- Regenerate rate: whether users use Less cringe / Witty / Warm refinement
- Perceived usefulness: 1-5 rating after session
- Repeat intent: "Would you use this again this week?"
- Cringe rate: % of replies marked too cringe

## Prompt quality rubric

A good reply is:

- short enough to send
- natural in real texting
- context-aware
- not generic
- not mean unless explicitly framed as safe banter
- not overly flirty or pickup-artist-like
- easy for the recipient to respond to

## Privacy baseline

The MVP has no login, no database, and no message storage. Messages are sent only to the server endpoint for generation.
